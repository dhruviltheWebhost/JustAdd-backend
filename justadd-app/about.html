About Us

We’re building a smart bridge between merchants who want to promote their business and freelancers looking to earn through digital work.

Merchants submit advertisements with details, budgets, and goals. Freelancers browse these ads and complete tasks like posting on social media, marketplaces, or groups.

After completing tasks, freelancers upload proof (like screenshots or links) to receive rewards. Our system also supports smart alerts, so freelancers can get notified when a new task matching their interest is live.

We make ad promotion easy, trackable, and rewarding — for everyone.
